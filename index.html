Featured Speakers

Admiral Michelle Howard

Retired U.S. Navy, First Female Four-Star Admiral

Breaking barriers throughout her 35-year career, Admiral Howard shares insights on leadership in challenging environments.

Colonel Jennifer Pritzker

Retired U.S. Army, Philanthropist

Founder of the Pritzker Military Museum & Library, Colonel Pritzker discusses preserving military history and supporting veteran communities.

Brigadier General Anne Macdonald

U.S. Army Reserve, Director of the VA Center for Women Veterans

Leading initiatives to improve healthcare and services for women veterans, General Macdonald shares the latest developments in veteran support.
